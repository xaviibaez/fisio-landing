## What is Mesotherapy?

Mesotherapy is a technique that consists of injecting small amounts of active substances directly into the middle layer of the skin (mesodermis). These injections contain a personalized combination of:

- **Vitamins** (A, C, E, B group)
- **Minerals** (zinc, silicon, magnesium)
- **Amino acids**
- **Lipolytic enzymes**
- **Hyaluronic acid**
- **Active compounds** specific for each treatment

## Available Treatments

### Cellulite Reduction
- Improves microcirculation
- Reduces fat nodules
- Smooths orange peel skin
- Visible results from the first sessions

### Localized Fat
- Lipolysis (fat dissolution)
- Body remodeling
- Common areas: abdomen, saddlebags, thighs, arms
