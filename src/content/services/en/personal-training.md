## What is Personal Training and Functional Recovery?

Personal training in the context of physiotherapy goes beyond simple physical exercise. It is a therapeutic approach that combines exercise with movement re-education to achieve complete functional recovery and prevent future injuries.

## Our services

### Movement Re-education

We work to correct dysfunctional movement patterns that can cause pain or injuries. Through specific exercises and body awareness techniques, we help you recover the quality of your movement.

- Correction of altered motor patterns
- Improved coordination and balance
- Body and proprioceptive awareness

### Sports Readaptation

Specialized program for safe return to sports activity after an injury. We design a gradual progression that respects recovery times and prepares the body for the specific demands of your sport.

- Progressive return to activity
- Sport-specific work
- Relapse prevention

### Abdominopelvic Readaptation

Specialized approach to recover the functionality of the abdominal and pelvic area. Ideal after pregnancy, abdominal surgeries, or to improve core performance.

- Postpartum recovery
- Diastasis recti treatment
- Functional core strengthening

### Gait Analysis for Runners

Detailed study of your running technique to identify areas for improvement and prevent injuries. We use expert observation to optimize your efficiency and reduce injury risk.

- Running biomechanics assessment
- Risk factor identification
- Guidelines for technique improvement

### Personal / Group Training

Training sessions supervised by the physiotherapist, individually or in small groups of maximum 2 people. Personalized attention guarantees correct exercise execution and maximum training effectiveness.

- Individual or paired sessions
- Constant supervision by the professional
- Adaptation to your needs and goals

## Who is it recommended for?

- People recovering from an injury
- Athletes who want to improve performance
- Runners looking to optimize their technique
- Postpartum women
- People with chronic pain
- Anyone looking for personalized attention in training
