## Kinesiotape (Neuromuscular Bandage)

Kinesiotape is an adhesive elastic bandage applied to the skin following the direction of the muscles. Its benefits include:

### How does it work?
- Provides muscle support without limiting movement
- Improves blood and lymphatic circulation
- Reduces pressure on pain receptors
- Facilitates postural correction

### Applications:
- Muscle and tendon injuries
- Edemas and hematomas
- Joint pain
- Postural support
- Sports recovery

## Dry Needling

Invasive technique that uses acupuncture needles to treat myofascial trigger points (painful muscle contractures).

### Benefits:
- **Immediate pain relief** in contracture areas
- **Deep muscle relaxation**
- **Improvement of joint mobility**
- **Treatment of referred pain** (radiating pain)

### Indications:
- Chronic muscle contractures
- Myofascial pain syndrome
- Neck and low back pain
- Tendinopathies
- Tension headaches
