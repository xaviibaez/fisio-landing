## Physiotherapy at Your Home

Home Physiotherapy brings all the benefits of professional treatment directly to your home. Our team of physiotherapists travels with the necessary equipment to offer you the same level of quality you would receive at the clinic, but with the added advantage of the comfort of your own environment.

## Who is it for?

### Elderly People
- Difficulty traveling to the clinic
- Better adherence to treatment at home
- Less stress and more comfort
- Home assessment to prevent falls

### Post-surgical Convalescence
- Recovery after operations
- Early mobilization at home
- Close follow-up in the critical phase
- Rehabilitation of hip, knee fractures, etc.

### Post-injury or Trauma
- Temporary immobilizations
- Injuries that hinder travel
- Intensive treatment in acute phase
- Care continuity
