## Origin and Foundations

Foot Reflexology is an ancient technique that comes from traditional Chinese medicine. It is based on the principle that the sole of the foot contains a complete map of the human body, with reflex zones corresponding to all organs and systems.

## How does it work?

The reflex zones of the foot are located in different nerve endings that, thanks to the Peripheral Nervous System, connect with:

- Respiratory system
- Digestive system
- Hormonal system
- Urinary system
- Musculoskeletal system
- Nervous system
- Immune system

## Therapeutic Benefits

The main objective is to **balance the physical and emotional body**, allowing:

- Relieve pain and muscle tension
- Improve blood and lymphatic circulation
- Reduce stress and anxiety
- Stimulate the body's self-healing capabilities
- Harmonize bodily functions
- Promote a state of deep relaxation
